Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your data

Last updated: January 2025

Dream Catcher Homes ("we", "our", "us") operates the website dream-catcherhomes.com. This policy explains how we handle your personal information when you visit our website or make a booking enquiry.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Name, email address, and phone number submitted via our enquiry form
  • Booking dates and villa preferences
  • Communication history via WhatsApp or email
  • Anonymous website usage data via cookies (e.g. pages visited, time spent)
  • IP address and browser type for security purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Respond to your booking enquiry or question
  • Process and manage your reservation
  • Send relevant updates about your stay
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Booking.com — for online reservations. Subject to Booking.com's Privacy Policy.
  • WhatsApp (Meta) — for direct communication. Subject to Meta's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Maps — for embedded location map. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — for typography. May log requests. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies to ensure basic functionality. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking beyond those listed above. You can disable cookies in your browser settings; however, this may affect some website features.

5. Data Retention

Enquiry data is retained for up to 2 years after your last contact or stay, unless you request earlier deletion. Booking records may be kept for up to 7 years for accounting and legal compliance.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority

7. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information. Our website uses HTTPS encryption. However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
